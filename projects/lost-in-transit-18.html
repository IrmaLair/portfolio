Lost in Transit

Storytelling · Jul, 2024 · 2 Weeks
This course explores the fundamental principles of immersive media through practical exercises, emphasizing mindfulness, collaboration, and storytelling. In the "Shadow Play Storytelling" project, students meditate to reflect on their day, extract a significant word, and collaboratively craft a script in groups. The stories come to life through shadow play, where students experiment with light, silhouettes, and motion to create immersive performances.
