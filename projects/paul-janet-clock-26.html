We all know how our perception of time changes as our life span increases. Most of us feel that time moved very slowly when we were younger and it gradually speeds up as we grow older. We usually become conscious of this speeding up when our lives become ordered into routines (routines make us lose sense of time).\n\nThis is exactly what Paul Janet's theory on time is about.
