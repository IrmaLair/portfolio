Kalpavriksh

XR Design · Feb, 2025 · 2 Weeks
Kalpavriksh is an interactive VR project where participants' professional goals and dreams take form as glowing energy, flowing into a living, evolving tree, inspired by Avatar’s Tree of Souls. Each aspiration aligns with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), symbolizing the power of collective ambition. By stepping into this immersive space, users witness how individual hopes merge into a shared ecosystem, fostering reflection, inspiration, and collaboration growing a tree of collective aspirations that push the world forward.
